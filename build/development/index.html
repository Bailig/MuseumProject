Developing a Story Map Strategy for Canada’s 150th Anniversary

This project creates a story map strategy with excellent simplicity and user experience that will help connect the Canada Science and Technology Museum with its audience in the future.

Project Background

As part of its groundbreaking plan of relaunching in 2017, Canada Science and Technology Museum requested a creation of geographic storytelling for the collections of Canada’s long and rich history of innovation in science and technology. The Project Respondent has been retained to develop a geotagged story map formation and implementation strategy, using geographic information technology, that will help guide the future development of an illustrative, interactive, and cost-efficient representation of the museum collections online.

Project Overview

Part One

- Student team from computer programming field

The first part of the project is to geocode the geographic names, such as street name and city name, to a latitude and longitude, which is done by the student team from computer programming field.

Input
Tables have a description with geographic names in each row.
Output
Tables with two columns of data added, which are geocoded latitude and longitude.

Part Two

- Bailig Abahanar, the GIS student

In the second part of the project, The Respondent created an interactive online map that manages to locate every invention and creation in technology and science throughout the history of Canada. The map utilizes the ESRI story map template and ArcGIS online.

Input
The data provided by the student team with latitude and longitude.
Output
A fully customized story map template;
A series of programming scripts for the preparation of the data required;
A practice guide on implementation of the story map template.

Project Benefits

Story Map Template

  • Gives a better understanding of what is a story map.
  • Introduces a methodology to enhance the digital storytelling.
  • Provides a fully customized story map template.

Script Tool

  • Helps to prepare the data that is going to be used for creating the story map.

Practice Guide

  • Guides the data preparation process for technical users who might take over the project.
  • Provides instructions on story map creation process with the story map customized template.
